# AgentRoadmap Product Vision

## Goal
To build a "100-Agent Platform" where visionary ideas are transformed into functional products through continuous, autonomous agent interaction.

## Vision Statement
Any visionary idea → shipped product, powered by an agent team that handles the work.

## The Human Role
The human role shifts from **doer to approver**. Humans provide the seed inspiration, budget, and final acceptance, while agents handle research, design, implementation, testing, and deployment.

## Core Values
1. **Agent-Native**: Designed from the ground up for AI agents, not just human developers.
2. **Speed & Scale**: Ultra-low latency coordination via SpacetimeDB to handle teams of 100+ agents.
3. **Auditability**: Complete history of every decision, discussion, and research step available in Git.
4. **Resilience**: Point-in-time recovery via hybrid storage (Database + Git).

## Roadmap Priorities
- **High Performance**: 150,000+ transactions per second for agent coordination.
- **Skill-Based Recruiting**: Automated agent selection based on task requirements and agent capabilities.
- **Economic Control**: Real-time budget management and resource allocation for every agent.
- **Self-Healing DAG**: Automated health monitoring, cycle detection, and orphan cleanup.
